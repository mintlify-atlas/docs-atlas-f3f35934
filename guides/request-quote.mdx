Reprodisseny reviews every quote request personally. Fill out the online form with your project details and you will receive a tailored quote within 24 business hours.

## How to submit a quote request

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the product you need">
    Navigate to the relevant product category on the Reprodisseny website — for example, large-format printing, PLV displays, vinyls, or packaging. Each product page has a **Request a quote** button that pre-fills the product name and category in the form.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill in your contact details">
    The form requires the following fields:

    - **Name** (required) — your full name, 2–80 characters.
    - **Email** (required) — a valid email address where you will receive the quote.
    - **Phone** (optional) — a direct number in case the team needs to clarify details quickly.
    - **Company** (optional) — your organization name. Useful for invoicing and for referencing your project internally.

    <Note>
      Your contact details are only used to process your quote request. Reprodisseny does not share this information with third parties.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Describe your project">
    Use the **Message** field to describe your specifications as precisely as possible. The more detail you provide, the more accurate the quote will be. Include:

    - Finished dimensions (width × height in mm or cm)
    - Quantity
    - Material or substrate preference (if you have one)
    - Finishing options — lamination, cutting, mounting, eyelets, etc.
    - Delivery deadline or urgency

    <Tip>
      Copy and paste your product datasheet or a brief into the message field if you have one ready. This saves a round-trip email.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Attach your file (optional)">
    You can attach an existing design file directly to the quote request. Accepted formats:

    | Format | Notes |
    |--------|-------|
    | PDF | Preferred for print-ready artwork |
    | JPEG | Photos and raster images |
    | PNG | Raster images with transparency |
    | SVG | Vector artwork |
    | EPS | Legacy vector format |
    | ZIP | Multiple files bundled together |

    Maximum file size: **25 MB**.

    If you are uploading a design file for production, leave the file type as **Design** (the default). Use **Brief** if you are sharing a reference document, or **Proof** if you are uploading a proof for approval.

    <Note>
      You do not need to attach a file to submit a quote request. You can send your files later once the project scope is agreed.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Accept the privacy policy and submit">
    Check the privacy policy consent box and click **Send request**. You will see a confirmation message on screen, and a copy of your submission is logged in the Reprodisseny system.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens next

After you submit the form:

1. **Within 24 business hours** — the Reprodisseny team reviews your request and any attached files.
2. **Pre-press check** — if you attached artwork, the team performs a preliminary check for obvious issues (color mode, resolution, bleed). They will flag any problems before producing a quote.
3. **Quote delivery** — you receive a detailed quote by email, including pricing, production lead time, and any file feedback.

<Info>
  Business hours are Monday to Friday, 08:00–18:00 CET. Requests submitted outside these hours are reviewed the next working day.
</Info>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How long does production take after I approve a quote?">
    Lead times vary by product and quantity. Standard small-format digital printing (flyers, business cards) is typically 2–3 business days. Large-format jobs, PLV displays, and custom packaging may take 5–10 business days. The quote will always include an estimated production time for your specific job.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if my file needs corrections after the quote?">
    If the pre-press check identifies issues — wrong color mode, missing bleed, low resolution — the team will contact you before production starts. You can upload a corrected file at no additional charge, provided the specifications have not changed. See the [file preparation guide](/guides/file-preparation) to avoid common issues.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I request an urgent order?">
    Yes. Note your deadline clearly in the **Message** field. Reprodisseny evaluates urgent requests on a case-by-case basis depending on current production capacity. An express surcharge may apply. Contacting the team directly by phone is the fastest way to confirm availability for same-day or next-day jobs.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
