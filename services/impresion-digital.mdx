Digital printing covers the broad range of printed communication materials used in marketing, sales, and corporate settings — from a business card to a full product catalog. Reprodisseny has been a pioneer in digital printing since 1993, when we installed the first digital black-and-white press in the studio. Today, our digital presses handle both short runs and high-volume production with consistent color quality across every sheet.

## Typical products

- **Brochures and dípticos**: Folded multi-page formats for product or service communication
- **Flyers and leaflets**: Single-sheet promotional pieces in any standard size
- **Business cards**: Standard or custom die-cut, with finishing options
- **Postcards and direct mail**: Print-ready or with variable data for personalized campaigns
- **Posters**: A0–A3 and custom sizes, suitable for framing or temporary display
- **Folders and dossiers**: Presentation folders with business card slits
- **Event programs and menus**: Short-run formatted documents for events
- **Corporate stationery**: Letterheads, compliment slips, envelopes

## Production equipment

Reprodisseny works with the leading digital press manufacturers:

- **Xerox iGen 5 and iGen 150**: High-volume production presses with exceptional color rendering and substrate flexibility. These presses support heavy coated stocks, textured papers, and clear toner effects.
- **Ricoh and Canon** digital presses: For versatile short and medium-run production across standard paper weights and finishes.

All production passes through individual color control and pre-press validation before printing.

## Variable data printing

We support **variable data printing (VDP)** — the ability to personalize each printed piece with unique text, names, codes, addresses, or images. This is used for:

- Personalized direct mail campaigns
- Event badges and tickets
- Loyalty cards with unique barcodes or QR codes
- Customized product documentation

<Note>
  Variable data files must include a structured data source (CSV or Excel) mapped to the template fields. Contact our team to discuss the setup before preparing your files.
</Note>

## Paper and finish options

| Finish | Description | Best for |
|---|---|---|
| **Gloss coated** | Bright, reflective surface | Product brochures, catalogs, posters |
| **Matte coated** | Soft, non-reflective surface | Reports, books, premium brochures |
| **Silk / satin** | Between gloss and matte | General marketing materials |
| **Uncoated** | Natural paper texture | Stationery, internal documents, sustainability-focused projects |
| **Textured / laid** | Embossed surface pattern | Premium invitations, corporate identity pieces |

### Post-press finishing

- Lamination: gloss, matte, soft-touch
- Spot UV varnish
- Folding: bi-fold, tri-fold, z-fold, roll-fold
- Saddle-stitching and perfect binding
- Die-cutting and creasing
- Numbering and perforating

## File requirements

- **Format**: PDF (recommended) — PDF/X-1a or PDF/X-4
- **Color mode**: CMYK; embed ICC profile if using custom color management
- **Bleed**: 3mm on all sides
- **Safety zone**: Keep all important text and logos at least 3–5mm inside the trim edge
- **Resolution**: 300 ppi for all raster images at final size
- **Fonts**: Convert to outlines or embed in the PDF before export
- **Black text**: Use 100% K only (avoid rich black on body text or fine type)
