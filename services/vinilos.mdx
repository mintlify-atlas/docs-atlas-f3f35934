Adhesive vinyls allow you to transform any surface into a communication medium — from a shopfront window to the full exterior of a vehicle. At Reprodisseny, we print, cut, and supply vinyls ready for professional installation, using materials and inks suited to each application surface and exposure level.

## Typical products

- **Window vinyls**: Full-color printed graphics or cut vinyls for glass
- **Frosted glass film**: Translucent adhesive for privacy partitions and branded dividers
- **Perforated vinyl (microperforado)**: Applied to glass — graphic visible from outside, transparent from inside
- **Floor decals**: Non-slip laminated floor graphics for retail, events, and wayfinding
- **Wall graphics**: Large-scale printed murals and branding for interior walls
- **Vehicle wraps**: Partial or full vehicle wraps, printed and laminated for outdoor durability
- **Structural vinyls**: Decorative cut vinyls for furniture, equipment, and branded surfaces
- **Label and sticker printing**: Short-run adhesive labels for product or packaging use

## Inks and environmental safety

All vinyl printing at Reprodisseny uses **HP latex inks** on HP latex plotters. Latex inks are water-based — they do not contain hazardous solvents and emit no harmful VOCs (volatile organic compounds). This makes them:

- Safe for installation in hospitals, schools, childcare facilities, and food environments
- Compliant with indoor air quality standards
- Suitable for graphics that come into contact with non-food surfaces
- Scratch-resistant and UV-stable for outdoor applications

<Info>
  If your installation is in a regulated environment (healthcare, food service), confirm material requirements with our team. We can specify vinyl and laminate combinations that meet the required standards.
</Info>

## Surface and material guide

| Application | Recommended vinyl type | Typical lifespan |
|---|---|---|
| Smooth glass (interior) | Cast vinyl, removable adhesive | 2–3 years |
| Textured wall | Polymeric vinyl, solvent-free | 3–5 years |
| Vehicle exterior | Cast vinyl + laminate | 5–7 years |
| Floor (pedestrian traffic) | Floor vinyl + anti-slip laminate | 1–2 years |
| Outdoor signage | Polymeric vinyl + UV laminate | 3–5 years |

## File preparation for vinyls

For best results, submit vector files for any vinyl that will be cut to shape. For printed vinyls, high-resolution raster files are accepted.

- **Preferred formats**: PDF (with outlines), AI, EPS for vector work
- **Color mode**: CMYK — avoid RGB, as screen colors will shift on print
- **Resolution**: 100–150 ppi at final print size for large-format vinyls; 300 ppi for small-format and labels
- **Cut paths**: Provide the cut contour on a clearly labeled separate layer
- **Dimensions**: Always specify the exact final size of the installed graphic, including any overlap or bleed onto adjacent surfaces
- **Bleed**: 5–10mm around the cut line for printed vinyls

<Warning>
  For vehicle wraps, provide the make, model, and year of the vehicle. Template files vary significantly between models and are essential for correct panel alignment.
</Warning>
