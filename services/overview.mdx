## How to choose the right service

Not sure which category fits your project? Use the table below as a quick reference.

| Need | Recommended service |
|---|---|
| Branded backdrop for an event | Large-Format Printing |
| In-store promotional stand | PLV and Point-of-Sale Displays |
| Window decoration or vehicle branding | Vinyls and Adhesive Graphics |
| Marketing brochures or business cards | Digital Printing |
| Product box or retail packaging | Packaging |
| Company catalog or annual report | Books and Publications |

<Info>
  All services are produced at our Juan de Mena production center in Barcelona. You can request pickup on-site or arrange delivery anywhere in Spain.
</Info>

## What sets us apart

Reprodisseny is not an online print shop or a traditional offset printer. We combine the flexibility and speed of digital production with personalized technical support on every project.

- **Color management**: Individual color control and validation before every production run
- **Custom solutions**: No fixed formulas — each project is scoped by use case, context, and budget
- **Technical expertise**: Over 40 years of experience with clients including Vall d'Hebron, Vueling, Hitachi, Renault, and the World Health Organization
- **End-to-end**: Design review, pre-press, production, finishing, and delivery
