Reprodisseny was born in 1983 in the Sant Gervasi neighbourhood of Barcelona as a small copy shop. Over four decades it has grown into one of Barcelona's leading digital printing companies, operating from a 1,000+ m² production centre and working with the world's top printing equipment manufacturers.

## Timeline

<Steps>
  <Step title="1983 — Foundation">
    Reprodisseny opens its doors in a 40 m² premises in the Sant Gervasi neighbourhood, offering copy and fast-print services.

    In 1986, the arrival of Macintosh computers and desktop-publishing software transformed the workflow — jobs that previously took weeks were completed in days.
  </Step>

  <Step title="1993 — First Digital Press">
    The Xerox Docutec black-and-white digital press marks the transition from analogue to digital production, setting the stage for the industry revolution to come.

    In 1996, Reprodisseny launches its first website.
  </Step>

  <Step title="1999 — Juan de Mena Production Centre">
    A pivotal milestone: the inauguration of the Centro de Producción Juan de Mena. Located near the Hospital de la Vall d'Hebron and just metres from the Ronda de Dalt ring road, the facility spans more than **1,000 m²** and gives the company the capacity to take on large-scale projects with speed and efficiency.
  </Step>

  <Step title="2006 — First Colour Digital Press">
    Reprodisseny becomes a pioneer in the sector by installing the **iGen 3**, the first high-volume colour digital press in its category.

    This consolidates the company's digital strategy. Reprodisseny is also the first in the sector to implement **Direct Smile**, a platform specialising in personalisation and multichannel communication.
  </Step>

  <Step title="2017 — Large-Format Expansion">
    Production capacity grows with the addition of two new colour presses — **iGen 150** and **iGen 5** — alongside a **Kongsberg** digital cutting table.

    Combined with **HP Latex** plotters and an in-house installation team, these investments allow Reprodisseny to take on large-format and large-scale projects across a fast-growing market segment.
  </Step>

  <Step title="2024 — 40 Years of Expertise">
    Celebrating four decades, Reprodisseny adds an **HP R1000** wide-format printer capable of printing on a wide variety of rigid materials.

    Today the company works with the leading machinery suppliers in the industry: **Ricoh, Canon, HP, Kongsberg, ESKO, Horizon,** and **Heidelberg**, and is established as one of Barcelona's best digital printing companies.

    A second revolution is beginning in the sector with the adoption of artificial intelligence.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Production Centre

The Centro de Producción Juan de Mena is the operational heart of Reprodisseny. Its more than 1,000 m² of floor space — located near the Vall d'Hebron area, with direct access to the Ronda de Dalt — are equipped to handle everything from short-run personalised jobs to high-volume production runs and large-format installations.
