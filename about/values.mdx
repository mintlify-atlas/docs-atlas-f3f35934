## Our Environmental Commitment

Sustainability is built into our day-to-day operations, not treated as an afterthought.

<Note>
  Reprodisseny holds FSC and PEFC certification, ensuring that paper products come from responsibly managed forests.
</Note>

Our environmental practices include:

- **FSC/PEFC certified papers** — sourced from responsibly managed forests, with recycled paper options available on request.
- **HP Latex inks** — water-based latex inks that are safe for the environment and for indoor use without off-gassing concerns.
- **Chemical recycling** — we recycle the chemical waste generated in our production processes.
- **Reactive energy reuse** — electrical energy from production equipment is recovered and reused where possible.
- **Paper consumption optimisation** — jobs are nested and scheduled to minimise substrate waste across every press run.

<Tip>
  If sustainability is a priority for your project, let us know when requesting a quote. We can recommend the most appropriate certified substrates and ink systems for your needs.
</Tip>
