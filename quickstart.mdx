## Get started in 3 steps

This guide walks you through placing your first order with Reprodisseny — from browsing our catalog to submitting a quote request with your print file.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find your product">
    Visit [reprodisseny.com](https://reprodisseny.com) and browse the product catalog. Products are organized by category:

    - **Gran formato** — Large-format banners, roll-ups, backdrops
    - **PLV y displays** — Point-of-sale displays, stands, totems
    - **Vinilos** — Adhesive vinyls, window graphics, floor decals
    - **Impresión digital** — Brochures, flyers, postcards, business cards
    - **Packaging** — Custom boxes and packaging
    - **Libros y revistas** — Books, catalogs, manuals

    Each product page shows available formats, materials, finishes, and common use cases.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Prepare your file">
    Before submitting your quote, make sure your artwork file is print-ready:

    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="Color mode">
        Work in **CMYK** for all print jobs. RGB files will be converted during pre-press, which may cause color shifts. For critical brand colors, specify Pantone references.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="Resolution">
        Images must be at least **300 ppi at final print size** for small/medium format. For large-format work viewed from a distance, 100–150 ppi at print size is acceptable.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="Bleed and margins">
        Add **3 mm of bleed** around all edges if your design extends to the cut line. Keep text and important elements at least 3–5 mm inside the trim edge.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="File format">
        Export as **PDF** with fonts embedded or converted to outlines. Flatten transparencies if you encounter RIP issues.
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>

    See the complete [file preparation guide](/guides/file-preparation) for detailed instructions.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit your quote request">
    Use the online quote form at [reprodisseny.com/pedir-presupuesto](https://reprodisseny.com/pedir-presupuesto):

    1. Enter your **name, email, and company** (optional)
    2. Select the **product** you need
    3. Add any **quantity, size, material, or finish** details in the message field
    4. Upload your **print file** (PDF, JPEG, PNG, SVG, ZIP, or EPS — up to 25 MB)
    5. Accept the **privacy policy** and submit

    You will receive a response within **24 business hours**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens after you submit

Once your quote request is received:

1. Our team reviews your specifications and file
2. If the file has pre-press issues, we contact you with corrections needed
3. You receive a detailed quote with pricing, timeline, and delivery options
4. Upon approval, we proceed to production
5. We notify you when your order is ready for pickup or dispatch

<Note>
  For urgent projects, contact us directly at [reprodisseny.com/contacto](https://reprodisseny.com/contacto) to discuss turnaround times.
</Note>

## Accepted file formats

| Format | Notes |
|--------|-------|
| PDF | Preferred. Embed fonts or convert to outlines. |
| JPEG / PNG | Accepted for photos and images. Minimum 300 ppi at print size. |
| SVG | Accepted for vector artwork. |
| ZIP | Use to bundle multiple files or linked assets. |
| EPS / AI | Accepted for vector files. Confirm compatibility with our team. |

<Warning>
  Avoid submitting files in Word (.docx) or PowerPoint (.pptx) for production work — layout shifts and font substitution can affect print quality. Always export to PDF.
</Warning>
